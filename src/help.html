FreshSweeper Instructions

How To Play

Click on a cell to reveal it. A revealed number indicates how many seeds are in the surrounding 8 cells. If you reveal a seed, the game is over and you have lost. You make mark a cell as having a seed with the 'F'

How To Win

Reveal all the cells that DO NOT have seeds.

Challenge Mode

In challenge mode, cells may have up to 3 seeds. You can cycle through how many seeds to mark by repeatedly pressing the 'F' key.
